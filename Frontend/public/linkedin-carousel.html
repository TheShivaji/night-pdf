I tried existing tools.

Most PDF dark mode tools had problems:

Broken formatting

Layouts would collapse or invert weirdly.

Poor readability

Low contrast made text harder to read.

Distorted images

Colors were inverted inside photos.

Privacy risks

Forced uploads to external servers.
